Delivering a seminar on mobile app development at a university in my hometown, in India
September 2016 (12th grade)

Near the beginning of my 12th grade year, Periyar Maniammai University (PMU), located in my family's hometown, invited me as a speaker to deliver a seminar to their CSE, ECE, & IT college students on mobile application development.

In this seminar, I not only discussed the approach to designing, developing, & distributing a mobile app, but also examined the factors that have made apps like Facebook and WhatsApp as successful as they currently are. I cherish this particularly memorable event, since it allowed me to share one of my deepest passions with other like-minded people!

Hosting a 2-day, hands-on Android app development workshop
November 2016 (12th grade)

After receiving very positive feedback from their students on my initial seminar at PMU, the university re-invited me to host a 2-day, hands-on Android app workshop.

In these 2 days, I took the students through the various stages of creating an actual app, including design, development, & distribution. IEEE's India chapter sponsored this awesome event!
